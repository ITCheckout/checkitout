About

Our goal here at ITCheckOut, is to make the process for students needing the lastest technologies simple and streamlined.

We provide Information Technology students with the devices on loan. Items include Apple laptops, Windows laptops, cameras, microphones, and lighting equipment.

Students are able to keep these devices for as long as the whole semester at free of charge.
